IT Interest

I personally like IT very much as it fascinates me everytime I come across it in every aspect be it productivity, application and its wide range of usage all over the world.

Things that really interests me in the field of IT will be mobile phones, automation, AI, holograms and so on and I really get fascinated by their evolution over time and for the future and I find them really interesting on how they can be so cool and help us so much in our lives.

I did a diploma in Electronic and Computer engineering previously in Singapore where I learnt some basic programming on C, C+,C++ and C# using the Visual studio Software and also to program Arduino boards and do linking of databases in the process.

I personally chose this IT course from RMIT online firstly because RMIT being well-recognised in the area of IT study and I believe it can help me strengthen and provide me a good foundation to help me continue my studies in the area of IT while studying this course online too can make it flexible in the midst of the busy schedule

Through this course, I hope to get good knowledge and understanding on the IT sector and the applications so that it can help me in pursuing a job in IT sector in the future. Also, I hope to gain more knowledge and experience from fellow classmates and the professors in the course and network and make friends as we share common interests in IT in this course.
